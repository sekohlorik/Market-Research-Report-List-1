<p><p>The absorbable surgical suture market is categorized into synthetic and natural sutures. Synthetic sutures include Poliglecaprone (Monocryl), Vicryl, and Polydioxanone (PDS), known for their tensile strength and predictability in absorption. Natural sutures comprise collagen and catgut, derived from biological sources, and are typically used for their biocompatibility. Polyglycolic acid sutures are also synthetic, offering quick absorption and effective wound support. These suture types cater to various surgical needs based on their properties, absorption rates, and intended applications.</p></p>
