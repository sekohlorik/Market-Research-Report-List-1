Injection grade polyketones are designed for processes like molding and provide high strength and impact resistance, making them ideal for complex part designs. In contrast, extrusion grade polyketones are tailored for continuous processing in applications like films and sheets, offering excellent flow properties and surface finish. Both grades are valued for their chemical resistance and versatility in various industrial applications, including automotive, packaging, and consumer goods.</p></p>
