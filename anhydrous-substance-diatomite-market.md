<p><p>The anhydrous substance diatomite market is segmented into three main types: natural, calcined, and flux calcined. Natural diatomite is unprocessed and retains its original properties, suitable for applications like filtration and insulation. Calcined diatomite is heated to remove moisture, enhancing its absorbent characteristics, making it ideal for construction and agriculture. Flux calcined diatomite undergoes a specific heating process to increase reactivity, serving specialized uses in industries such as pharmaceuticals and food processing, where purity and functionality are crucial.</p></p>
