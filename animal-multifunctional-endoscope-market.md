<p><p>The Animal Multifunctional Endoscope market comprises two main types: rigid and flexible endoscopes. Rigid endoscopes are designed with a fixed structure, ideal for examining areas that require stability, such as joints or body cavities in larger animals. In contrast, flexible endoscopes feature a pliable design, allowing for easy navigation through tortuous anatomical pathways, making them suitable for a variety of diagnostic and therapeutic procedures in animals. Both types play crucial roles in veterinary medicine and diagnostics.</p></p>
