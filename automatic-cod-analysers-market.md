<p><strong>The Automatic COD Analysers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Potassium Dichromate Method</li><li>Potassium Permanganate Method</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic COD (Chemical Oxygen Demand) Analysers market comprises several methods, primarily the Potassium Dichromate Method and the Potassium Permanganate Method. The Potassium Dichromate Method is widely used for measuring organic compounds and requires the oxidation of samples in strong acidic conditions, providing accurate COD readings. The Potassium Permanganate Method, on the other hand, is suitable for less toxic compounds and operates in neutral or slightly acidic conditions. Additionally, "Others" refers to alternative methods like UV photometry and various titrimetric techniques, offering diverse analytical approaches for COD measurement.</p></p>
