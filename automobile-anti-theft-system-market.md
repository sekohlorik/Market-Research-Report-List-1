<p><p>The automobile anti-theft system market comprises various technologies designed to enhance vehicle security. Steering locks physically prevent the steering wheel from turning, while alarms deter theft through loud alerts. Immobilizers disable the engine without the correct key, preventing unauthorized operation. Remote keyless entry allows users to unlock and start their vehicle without physical keys, enhancing convenience. Central locking secures all doors simultaneously, and biometric capture devices use fingerprint or facial recognition for personalized access, elevating security measures against theft.</p></p>
