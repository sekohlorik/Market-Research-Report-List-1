<p><p>The Automotive Dual Variable Valve Timing (VVT) market encompasses various types based on timing mechanisms of the intake valves. Late Intake Valve Closing (LIVC) improves efficiency by delaying the closing of the intake valves, thus enhancing cylinder filling and reducing emissions. Early Intake Valve Closing (EIVC) aids in combustion control and fuel efficiency by closing valves earlier. Early Intake Valve Opening (EIVO) allows more air to enter the cylinder, boosting performance. Other variants include advanced adjustments to optimize engine output and efficiency.</p></p>
