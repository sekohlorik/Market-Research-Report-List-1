<p><p>The automotive engine management sensors market encompasses various types, essential for optimizing engine performance and efficiency. Crankshaft sensors monitor crankshaft position for accurate timing, and camshaft sensors ensure proper valve operation. Pressure sensors gauge various pressures within the engine, and temperature sensors track heat levels. These sensors are crucial for enhancing engine control, fuel efficiency, and emissions management in modern vehicles.</p></p>
