<p><p>The automotive speed radar market encompasses three primary technologies: Laser, Ultrasonic, and Microwave. </p><p>Laser technology uses focused beams to measure speed with high accuracy over long distances. Ultrasonic technology employs sound waves to detect speed, often in close-range applications. Microwave technology utilizes radio waves, allowing for effective speed measurement at various distances and in diverse conditions. Each type offers unique advantages, catering to different applications and requirements in traffic enforcement, vehicle safety systems, and automotive diagnostics.</p></p>
