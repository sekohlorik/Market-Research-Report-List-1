<p><p>Balloon dilatation catheters are specialized medical devices used to widen narrowed or obstructed blood vessels. The market for these catheters is primarily divided into two types: semicompliant balloon dilatation catheters and other types. Semicompliant balloons are designed to expand under controlled pressure, allowing for precise dilation and minimizing the risk of vessel rupture. Other types may include compliant balloons, which are more flexible and adapt to vessel contours, or non-compliant balloons that maintain a fixed diameter during inflation, each serving specific clinical needs.</p></p>
