<p><strong>The Brass Solenoid Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normally Open</li><li>Normally Closed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brass solenoid valve market is segmented into two main types: normally open and normally closed valves. Normally open valves allow fluid flow when de-energized and close when energized, making them ideal for applications requiring flow interruption. Conversely, normally closed valves block fluid flow when de-energized and open on energization, suited for systems needing fail-safe operation. These valves cater to diverse industries, including irrigation, HVAC, and automation, influencing design choices based on specific operational requirements and safety considerations.</p></p>
