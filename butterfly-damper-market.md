<p><p>The butterfly damper market is categorized into three main types: manual, pneumatic, and electric. The manual type relies on hand-operated mechanisms for adjusting airflow, making it cost-effective and simple. Pneumatic dampers use air pressure for operation, allowing for quick response and precise control, often utilized in industrial applications. Electric dampers are powered by electric actuators, providing automated control and integration with building management systems, ideal for modern HVAC solutions, enhancing efficiency and user convenience.</p></p>
