<p><p>The Cadmium Zinc Telluride (CZT) crystal market is primarily categorized into monocrystalline wafers and monocrystalline ingots. Monocrystalline wafers are thin, flat slices of CZT used in various applications such as sensors and detectors due to their uniform properties and efficiency. Monocrystalline ingots, on the other hand, are larger, bulk forms of CZT that are further processed into wafers. These types serve distinct purposes in sectors like medical imaging, nuclear detection, and renewable energy, highlighting the versatility of CZT.</p></p>
