<p><p>The Central Negative Pressure Suction Pump market is categorized into two main types: dry smoking and wet pumping. Dry smoking pumps utilize a vacuum system to create suction without the presence of fluids, making them ideal for applications requiring minimal contamination. In contrast, wet pumping systems operate with a medium, typically a liquid, to facilitate suction and enhance efficiency in fluid transfer processes. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, including healthcare, pharmaceuticals, and industrial applications.</p></p>
