<p><p>Ceramic dielectric powders are classified into various types based on their composition and applications. X7R powders offer good capacitance stability over a wide temperature range, making them suitable for decoupling applications. COG powders exhibit very low dielectric loss and temperature coefficients, ideal for high-frequency circuits. Y5V powders provide higher capacitance but with more variability under temperature and voltage, often used in consumer electronics. Other types include various formulations tailored for specific electronic applications, enhancing performance and reliability.</p></p>
