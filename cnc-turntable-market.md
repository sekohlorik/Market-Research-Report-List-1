<p><strong>The CNC Turntable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Axis</li><li>Biaxial</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CNC turntable market encompasses two primary types: single-axis and biaxial. Single-axis turntables enable rotation around a single pivot point, ideal for applications requiring precise, linear motion in one direction. In contrast, biaxial turntables offer rotation across two axes, facilitating more complex machining processes and allowing for multi-dimensional workpieces. These types cater to various industries, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in manufacturing by enabling automated and programmable operations tailored to specific machining requirements.</p></p>
