<p><ul><li>Absorbable Membrane</li><li>Non-absorbable Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental barrier membrane bone graft market is categorized into two main types: absorbable and non-absorbable membranes. Absorbable membranes are designed to dissolve over time, gradually being replaced by natural tissue, making them suitable for one-time procedures and reducing the need for surgical removal. In contrast, non-absorbable membranes provide long-term support, remaining in place until replaced or removed surgically. Each type serves specific dental applications, such as guided bone regeneration, influencing the choice based on treatment requirements and patient needs.</p></p>
