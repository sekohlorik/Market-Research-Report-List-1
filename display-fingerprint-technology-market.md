<p><ul><li>Optical Identification</li><li>Ultrasound Identification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Display fingerprint technology encompasses two main types: optical identification and ultrasound identification. Optical identification uses light to capture finger images through the display, allowing for quick and cost-effective integration into smartphones and devices. In contrast, ultrasound identification employs sound waves to analyze the unique characteristics of a fingerprint beneath the skin's surface, enhancing accuracy and security. Both technologies cater to the growing demand for biometric authentication solutions, balancing user convenience with robust security features in digital devices.</p></p>
