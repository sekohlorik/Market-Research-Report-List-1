<p><ul><li>Tandem Mass Spectrometry</li><li>Immunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The endocrine testing market primarily comprises two key types: tandem mass spectrometry and immunoassays. Tandem mass spectrometry offers high sensitivity and specificity for measuring hormones and metabolites, making it suitable for complex samples. This method is often used for newborn screening and monitoring therapeutic drug levels. Immunoassays, on the other hand, utilize antibodies to detect hormones in blood samples, providing rapid results for conditions like diabetes and thyroid disorders. Both techniques are essential for accurate hormonal assessment and disease diagnosis.</p></p>
