<p><strong>The Fire Duct Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive</li><li>Positive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire duct systems can be categorized into two main market types: passive and positive. Passive fire duct systems focus on preventing the spread of fire and smoke through containment measures, utilizing materials and designs that resist flames and maintain structural integrity during a fire event. In contrast, positive fire duct systems are designed to actively manage smoke and heat, employing mechanical systems to control airflow and enhance safety. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring fire safety in buildings.</p></p>
