<p><p>High-performance rare earth magnets are essential components in various applications, particularly in Voice Coil Motors (VCM). The market for these magnets includes two main types: sintered rare earth magnets and bonded rare earth magnets. </p><p>Sintered rare earth magnets are created by compacting and sintering powdered materials, offering high magnetic strength and thermal stability, making them ideal for demanding applications. Bonded rare earth magnets, on the other hand, are formed by combining magnetic powders with a polymer matrix, providing flexibility in shapes and sizes while maintaining decent magnetic performance.</p></p>
