<p><p>The Indium Oxide Sputtering Target market is divided into two main types: Rotatable and Non-Rotating targets. Rotatable Indium Oxide targets enhance film uniformity and deposition efficiency by allowing continuous rotation during the sputtering process. This type is ideal for large-area coatings. In contrast, Non-Rotating targets are typically stationary, making them suitable for specific applications where precise control over deposition thickness is required. The choice between these types depends on the application's scalability, uniformity needs, and substrate size.</p></p>
