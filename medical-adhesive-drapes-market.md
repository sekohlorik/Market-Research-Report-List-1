Negative pressure wound therapy drapes are designed to create a sealed environment around a wound, facilitating healing by applying negative pressure and removing exudate. Surgical drapes, on the other hand, are utilized during surgical procedures to maintain a sterile field and protect the patient and healthcare staff from contamination. Both types serve essential roles in enhancing patient care and optimizing clinical outcomes.</p></p>
