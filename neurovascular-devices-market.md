<p><ul><li>Aneurysm Coiling & Embolization Devices</li><li>Cerebral Balloon Angioplasty & Stenting Systems</li><li>Support Devices</li><li>Neurothrombectomy Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The neurovascular devices market encompasses various categories aimed at treating cerebrovascular conditions. Aneurysm coiling and embolization devices are used to block blood flow to aneurysms, preventing rupture. Cerebral balloon angioplasty and stenting systems restore blood flow by dilating narrowed arteries and providing support. Support devices, such as guiding catheters, assist in the delivery of other devices. Neurothrombectomy devices are designed to remove blood clots from the brain's vessels, enhancing blood flow and reducing the risk of stroke.</p></p>
