<p><ul><li>Cryogenic Distillation</li><li>Molecular Sieve Adsorption Method</li><li>Membrane Permeation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oxygen plants utilize various technologies for oxygen production, primarily including cryogenic distillation, molecular sieve adsorption, and membrane permeation. Cryogenic distillation involves cooling air to separate oxygen from nitrogen based on boiling points. Molecular sieve adsorption uses porous materials to selectively adsorb nitrogen, concentrating oxygen. Membrane permeation employs selective membranes that allow oxygen to pass while blocking nitrogen, offering a compact and energy-efficient solution. Each method caters to different industrial needs, balancing efficiency, cost, and scalability in oxygen supply.</p></p>
