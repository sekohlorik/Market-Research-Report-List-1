<p><p>The panic attack treatment market encompasses various medication types, each targeting specific neurotransmitter systems. Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitors (SSRIs) primarily enhance serotonin levels, promoting mood stability. Serotonin Norepinephrine Reuptake Inhibitors (SNRIs) elevate both serotonin and norepinephrine, addressing anxiety more comprehensively. Benzodiazepines (BZDs) offer rapid relief from acute anxiety symptoms by enhancing GABA activity, although they carry risks of dependency. These treatment options cater to different patient needs and response profiles in managing panic attacks effectively.</p></p>
