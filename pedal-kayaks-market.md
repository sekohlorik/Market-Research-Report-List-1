<p><ul><li>Push Pedal</li><li>Rotational Pedal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pedal kayak market features distinct types of propulsion systems: push pedal and rotational pedal. Push pedal systems employ a straightforward back-and-forth motion, allowing for efficient forward movement. They are often favored for their simplicity and ease of use in calm waters. In contrast, rotational pedal systems utilize a circular pedaling motion, similar to a bicycle, enabling increased speed and maneuverability. Both types cater to different user preferences, enhancing the kayaking experience by providing alternative ways to navigate waterways.</p></p>
