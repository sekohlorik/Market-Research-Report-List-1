<p><p>The Peltier cooler market is categorized into single-stage and multi-stage modules. In contrast, multi-stage modules offer enhanced cooling capabilities by stacking multiple thermoelectric elements, making them suitable for high-performance applications, including laser cooling and sensor systems. Both types serve distinct market needs, with single-stage modules focusing on simplicity and cost-effectiveness, while multi-stage modules cater to specialized cooling demands.</p></p>
