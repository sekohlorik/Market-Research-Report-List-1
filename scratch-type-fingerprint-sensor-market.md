<p><p>The Scratch-type Fingerprint Sensor market is categorized into two main types: Direct Detection and Reflective Detection. Direct Detection sensors capture fingerprint features by directly analyzing the skin's surface, which allows for high accuracy and detail in identifying unique patterns. Reflective Detection, on the other hand, employs light reflection to sense fingerprints, often benefiting from reduced sensitivity to surface contaminants. Each type serves distinct applications, emphasizing either precision or adaptability, thereby influencing their use in security systems and consumer devices.</p></p>
