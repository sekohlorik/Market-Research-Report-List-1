<p><p>The Special Copper Clad Laminate market encompasses various materials with distinctive properties. BT/epoxy fiberglass boards offer high thermal stability and electrical insulation. Modified FR-4 is known for low coefficient of thermal expansion (CTE) and low dielectric constant/dissipation factor (Dk/Df), enhancing performance. PPO modified epoxy boards provide improved moisture resistance. BT-like boards mimic traditional BT materials with enhanced features. PTFE boards excel in low dielectric losses. Hydrocarbon boards are lightweight and eco-friendly. PI/glass fiber boards combine superior heat and chemical resistance. Other segments include niche applications catering to specific industry needs.</p></p>
