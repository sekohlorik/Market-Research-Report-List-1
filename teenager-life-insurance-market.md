<p><p>The teenager life insurance market is categorized into three main types: survival insurance, death insurance, and full insurance. Survival insurance provides benefits if the insured outlives the policy term, offering savings for future needs. Death insurance pays out a predetermined sum to beneficiaries upon the policyholder's death, ensuring financial security for loved ones. Full insurance combines elements of both survival and death coverage, providing a payout for either scenario, catering to diverse financial planning needs for families with teenagers.</p></p>
