<p><strong>The Three-phase Distribution Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Type</li><li>Oil Immersed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The three-phase distribution transformer market primarily consists of two types: dry type and oil-immersed type. Dry type transformers utilize air as the cooling medium, offering lower maintenance and enhanced safety, making them suitable for indoor applications. In contrast, oil-immersed transformers use oil for cooling and insulation, providing better efficiency and higher power capacity, ideal for outdoor use. Both types serve crucial roles in electrical distribution, with specific advantages tailored to different environmental conditions and operational requirements.</p></p>
