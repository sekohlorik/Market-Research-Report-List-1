<p><p>Torque limiting clutches are essential devices used to protect machinery by preventing excessive torque transmission. The market is categorized into several types. Friction-type clutches utilize friction materials to slip at a predetermined torque, ensuring safety in operation. Ball-type clutches employ balls that engage or disengage based on torque levels, providing a precise torque cutoff. Other types may include magnetic or roller clutches, each designed for specific applications. These mechanisms enhance reliability and extend the lifespan of machinery by preventing overload conditions.</p></p>
