<p><p>The Vagus Nerve Stimulation (VNS) devices market is categorized into two primary types: implantable VNS devices and external VNS devices. Implantable devices are surgically placed under the skin and deliver electrical impulses to the vagus nerve, helping to treat conditions like epilepsy and depression. In contrast, external VNS devices are non-invasive and are typically worn externally, providing stimulation without surgery. Both types aim to improve patient outcomes by modulating nerve activity to manage various neurological and psychiatric disorders.</p></p>
