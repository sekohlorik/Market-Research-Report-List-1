<p><p>The veterinary ECG systems market comprises several key segments. Resting ECGs are used for immediate cardiac assessments in animals, allowing veterinarians to monitor heart rhythms during examinations. Holter ECGs provide continuous monitoring over 24 hours or more, capturing transient arrhythmias and offering a comprehensive view of heart activity during normal daily behaviors. Other market types include telemetry systems and event recorders, catering to specialized diagnostic needs and enabling remote monitoring of an animal's cardiac health in various clinical settings.</p></p>
