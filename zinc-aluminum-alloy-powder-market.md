<p><p>The Zinc Aluminum Alloy Powder market can be categorized into two primary types: flake and spherical. Flake powders, characterized by their flat, thin particles, are commonly used in applications requiring high surface area and excellent oxidation resistance, like coatings and thermal spray. In contrast, spherical powders feature round particles, which enhance flowability and packing density, making them ideal for additive manufacturing and injection molding processes. Each type serves distinct industrial needs based on their physical properties and applications.</p></p>
